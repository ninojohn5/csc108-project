Knight's Tour Challenge

Guide the knight across the board, visiting every square exactly once using its unique L-shaped movement. Plan your moves carefully to complete the tour!

Game Rules

  • Objective: Visit all squares on the board exactly once.
  • Movement: The knight moves in an L-shape: Two squares in one direction, then one square perpendicular (or vice versa).
  • Rules:
    • No revisiting squares.
    • You can adjust the board size.
    • You can undo moves.
    • You can use Auto-Play to finish the game.
    • You can paused/resumed auto playing mode.
    • You can reset the game anytime.
    • You can customize the color of the board by clicking 'Customize Board' in the main menu.
